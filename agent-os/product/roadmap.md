# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP

- Multi-hotel coordination and alerts — cross-property strategy execution with anomaly detection
- Campaign management and reporting — campaign setup, performance tracking, and reporting
- Content management — manage ad content across hotels and campaigns
- Budget management — budget allocation and control across the portfolio

## Phase 2: Post-Launch

- AI-driven automation — intelligent bidding, forecasting, and strategy recommendations
- Automated / AI-driven campaign setup — reduce manual effort in launching new campaigns
- Expanded media types and channels — extend beyond paid search to additional digital channels
